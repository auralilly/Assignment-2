SYNOPSIS

New York cop John McClane flies to Los Angeles on Christmas Eve to reconcile with his estranged wife. When German terrorists seize the Nakatomi Plaza skyscraper, McClane becomes the only hope for the hostages — barefoot, outgunned, and ready to deliver the ultimate holiday gift: justice.
